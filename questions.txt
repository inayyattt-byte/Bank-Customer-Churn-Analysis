1. Which country has highest churn?
Answer:
Germany had the highest customer churn rate at approximately 22%.

2. Do inactive members leave more?
Answer:
Active members showed a slightly higher churn rate compared to inactive members in this dataset.

3. Which age group exits most?
Answer:
Customers aged 60+ showed the highest churn rate, followed closely by customers aged 46–60.

4. Do higher balances affect churn?
Answer:
Customers in Germany had the highest average account balance, and Germany also showed the highest churn rate, suggesting a possible relationship between higher balances and churn.

Answer:
Female customers demonstrated a slightly higher churn rate compared to male customers.
